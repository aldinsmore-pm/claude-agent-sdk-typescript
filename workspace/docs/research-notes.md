# Research notes

## Market summary
- Competitors focus on search, but lack strong "generate" workflows.
- Users value transparency and saved artifacts over chat logs.

## Customer quotes
- "I need something I can hand to my team, not another chat transcript."
- "Show me what changed so I can trust the output."

## Metrics to watch
- Time from prompt to saved brief
- Re-run rate (iteration)
- Documents created per workspace
